Mouse and Cheese

It is a word's spelling Game. Basically there is a mouse on the floor with a piece of cheese to the right.
Your goal is to not let the mouse get to the cheese.
At the start of the game, you will be provided with several empty underlines in a line.
You start by guessing what letter is in the word.
Once you start, there is a board that stores the "guess history" of the wrong letter if the letter doesn't exist.
If it does exist in the word, the letter would be put on the underline. For each wrong guess, the mouse would move to right.
If the mouse reaches the cheese, you lose.

After you win or lose, you can restart the game by clicking the "Restart" button and then click "Start".
